Bangladesh Olympiadians Club

Our organization "Bangladesh Olympians Club" mainly provides students with various information about different national and international Olympiads. In addition, the club organizes various events for your practice. At present, there are about 10,000 students from different districts of Bangladesh in the group of "Olympians Club in Bangladesh". Our goal is one - to inform the whole of Bangladesh about the various national and international Olympiads and to make the students interested in it.
